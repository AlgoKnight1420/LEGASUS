About RFID Tag

Each LEGASUS product comes with a unique RFID tag that ensures its authenticity. This tag contains a unique identifier that is registered in our database when the product is manufactured.

The RFID tag is embedded in the product and cannot be removed or tampered with without damaging the product. This ensures that the product you receive is genuine and has not been counterfeited.
